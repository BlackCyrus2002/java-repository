Keep an eye on your account

Checks, receipts or credit card, all expenses must be accumulated and placed opposite income, and anticipated future debits in order to maintain a fund of sufficient turnover on the account.

Notify us in case of overdraft

Faced with an unforeseen expense or a drop in income, turning your back useless. It is best to explain the situation to your banker, or even to adapt the amount of the authorized overdraft. When you stay in this envelope, the amount of the agios is fixed by the account agreement.

Automate your banking operations

Transfers or direct debits, it is in your interest to automate the operations on your account in order to collect your salary as quickly as possible. as soon as possible and to schedule payments (payment of invoices, etc.) at the time most favorable to you. Paying the direct debits monthly smooths them out over time. This avoids accordion effects complicates account management.

Open a savings account to smooth your cash flow

If you can, open a savings account (Livret A, etc.) and pay into it a sum of X FCFA every month. Thus, you can make a transfer to credit your bank account and avoid the payment of agios in the event of an overdraft. When your salary has fallen, all you have to do is transfer the same amount to the savings account to replenish your kitty

Be careful

Nobody needs to know the Pin code of your bank card. In case of theft, a deductible of 150,000 FCFA will be applied on the sums debited before the opposition of the card if the transactions were made with the code. Likewise, it is best to keep your means of payment in a safe place.

Use bank alerts

Some establishments offer an alert system. It inventories the account balance and triggers the sending of SMS to the holder's mobile to warn him if his account goes into the red, etc.
